A Brief history of the Pendola Power Rankings

It all started one day in Fall quarter of 2015, because of a comment made by James, who ironically avoided the ranking system for the next several months. Are you happy now? I hope so.

He noted that we were not salty enough, and proposed that we keep rankings in order to become saltier. His brainchild developed into the quest to find a suitable ranking system, led by mathematicians Thomas (2%LF) and Dylan (asdf). Thomas was the first to invent his own shitty ELO system.

It quickly became apparent that this system was too volatile, and so we dubbed it the salt maker ranking, and supplemented our competitive scene with a standard ELO system. The salt maker rankings eventually became completely broken.

Then, one day, at a table in the Ortega dining commons, while staring at 2%LF's face, I decided that building this system would make for a nice project. And here we are. You're welcome. Special thanks to Kenny for his help.
